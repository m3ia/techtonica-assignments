Grilled Tequila Lime Sweet Potato Wedges

A savory treat for the snack-loving programmer

By Melissa Natividad

Posted: July 27, 2022

The first time I had Kinders Tequila Lime Sweet Potato Fries was in college in my hometown. A lot of school athletes would get sandwiches at this well-known local bbq spot, which I did not care for, until I tried it without any other option. I've gained many-a-pound since then.

What You'll Need:

  • 2-3 small sweet potatoes
  • 3 Tablespoons olive or neutral oil
  • 2 teaspoons Kinder's Tequila Lime Rub

Instructions

  1. Scrub sweet potatoes under running water, dry and cut lengthwise into 8 wedges.
  2. Place wedges in a bowl and combine with oil and Kinder's Tequila Lime Rub
  3. Preheat grill, if using charcoal grill, set up for direct & indirect cooking.
    4. Grill sweet potato wedges on medium low heat, flipping every 5 minutes, for about
  4. 20 minutes, or until soft.
  5. Remove from grill. Serve hot.
